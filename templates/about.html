About EduConnect

Our Mission

At EduConnect, we believe that education should be accessible, organized, and collaborative. Our platform bridges the gap between educators and students, providing a centralized hub for sharing resources, hosting live sessions, and fostering a thriving academic community across multiple departments and years of study.

What We Do

  • Resource Management: Allowing teachers to securely upload notes, syllabi, and study materials specific to each class.
  • Live Sessions: Native integration for creating and managing live interactive classrooms with automated attendance tracking.
  • Institutional Scale: Built from the ground up to support multiple colleges, departments, and varying academic years.

Join Us

Whether you are an institution looking to modernize your teaching infrastructure, an educator wanting a better way to reach your students, or a student striving for academic excellence, EduConnect is built for you.
