Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account (name, email, role), update your profile, or communicate with us. We also automatically record your interaction with the platform (e.g., last active timestamps, live session attendance).

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Attendance data is shared securely with your educators. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Passwords are cryptographically hashed and never stored in plain text.
